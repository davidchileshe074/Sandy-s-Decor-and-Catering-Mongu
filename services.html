Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, we offer comprehensive packages that include both luxury decor and premium catering to ensure a seamless experience for your event.

How far in advance should I book my event?

We recommend booking at least 3 to 6 months in advance, especially for weddings and large corporate events, to guarantee availability and allow time for design planning.
